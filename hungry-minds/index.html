About Us

We are a group of game developers whose passion lies in creating fun and creative games to educate and entertain children all around the world in various subjects such as English, Mathematics, Science and more. As a studio, we aim to make game experiences that are easily digestible to our target audience as they navigate the worlds we make within the confines of the student's place of study, whether it be in the classroom or in their tutelage.
